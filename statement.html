Artist Statements

My artworks are all about digital medial art. Including glitch art,composite art,sound art,video art,net art,and code art. The materials that I used are Photoshop, Atom, Github, Vimeo,Soundcloud, and P5. My artworks address that how those materials and technique relates to our medial world.

The glitch art, sound art, video art, and net art are reflected my daily life. The images that I took during my vacation, the sounds I recorded during the school, the video I recorded from my way to home. The composite art and net art are worked with my classmates. We created really nice artworks together. For my final project, I used Github and Atom to created a video game named Whack-a-mole.
