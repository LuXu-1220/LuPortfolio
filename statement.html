Artist Statements

Your artist statement should be approximately 250 words long. Remember that the artist statement is a chance to tell the reader what you do and why you do it. Start from where you are- speak directly and clearly and be honest. It’s okay if you’re just starting to make art and your statement is shorter. You’re just getting started. 250 words is usually two paragraphs:

First Paragraph

Identify the medium or mediums you work in and what process you use. Identify your reason for making work and for using the materials that you use. Also address other issues that are important (why do you do what you do? how does it connect to the outside world?)

Second Paragraph
